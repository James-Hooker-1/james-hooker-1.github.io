Let go or be dragged.

XUR is a CT-based experimental music band.

The band consists of James Hooker on guitar and vocals, Henry Levitt on bass, Matteo Bontempi on drums, and Megan Greenberg on saxophone.



Shot by Austin Corley in Champaign, Urbana, IL, Earth.

Our latest album, Deliverance, was written somewhere between Hartford and Kalamazoo, recorded over the summer, and released on November 17, 2024 through Be Here Sound and YAP Records. Mixed by me, with pointers from Louie and Conlan. Art by my brother Will, and featuring Noah Sautner on Boefinger. Thank you to the moon and the planets. The rushing wind caressing a valley, telephone poles creaking under their own weight. The orange glow of street-lamps at night; an old, dusty book - eyes gently opening.
