A line or so about God

Back

June 2024


A line or so about God

and hanging my head upside 

down on the park bench

to see a crescent moon

through the trees

working out a verse

about writing this down 

in the dark and giving

something up to something

else.


I broke off into conversation

and struggle to 

recapture the theme:

You can see the dark 

parts of the moon too

Like, leaving it all

up to God.

Or rather passing off

all responsibility for my

handwriting.


for example

how my faith was

stronger in the dark

picking up a phrase

or two like antique

jewelry 

measuring, thinking,

casting it off

Or, 

wandering around the

forest humming, 

& unable to

conceptualize footsteps.

Meaning dissolves in the light.
